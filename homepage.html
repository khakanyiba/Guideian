Your Study Roadmap

A step-by-step plan to prepare for your recommended programs and career path.

Current Year (Grade 12)

Focus on Academic Excellence

  • Maintain strong grades in Mathematics (target 85%+) and Physical Sciences (target 80%+)
  • Prepare for final exams with structured study plan
  • Research university application deadlines and requirements
  • Attend university open days (virtual or in-person)
Next 6 Months

University Applications

  • Apply to 3-5 recommended universities before closing dates
  • Prepare for and write NBT tests if required
  • Apply for financial aid and scholarships (NSFAS, bursaries)
  • Consider early application to increase chances
First Year University

Foundation Building

  • Core courses: Mathematics, Computer Science, Physics
  • Join relevant student societies (Computer Science Society, Engineering Club)
  • Attend career fairs and networking events
  • Maintain strong GPA (target 70%+)
  • Explore research opportunities with professors
Second Year University

Specialization Begins

  • Begin specialization courses based on interests
  • Apply for summer internships in tech/engineering
  • Consider undergraduate research projects
  • Develop programming portfolio with class projects
Third Year University

Career Preparation

  • Complete advanced coursework in chosen specialization
  • Apply for graduate programs if considering further study
  • Attend recruitment events and apply for graduate positions
  • Complete capstone project demonstrating skills
