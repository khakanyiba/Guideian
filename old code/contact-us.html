Frequently Asked Questions

We hope this FAQ section has addressed some of your common questions. If you have any further queries, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

How do I know which courses are right for me?

We offer personalized course recommendations based on your interests, academic strengths, and career goals. Our education advisors can help assess your profile and suggest programs that align with your aspirations.

Does Guideian handle university applications for me?

Yes, we provide comprehensive university application support. Our team can help with preparing documents, meeting deadlines, and navigating the entire application process for South African universities.

How does Subject Selection Support work?

Our Subject Selection Support service helps high school students choose the right subjects that align with their future university and career goals. We consider your strengths, interests, and potential university requirements.
